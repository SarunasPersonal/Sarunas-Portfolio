Data Principles and Database Development

Data Principles and Database Development provided me with the perfect opportunity to delve deeper into my interest in databases, data structures, and SQL. This unit tought how to effectively deploy databases. I gained a comprehensive understanding of database creation, including tables, views, stored procedures, and functions.

First Assignment

The first assignment served as an excellent introduction to the world of databases. Our objective was to create a presentation focused on the role of data in e-commerce. As part of our research, we discovered an interesting fact that WH Smith made the first-ever e-commerce sale. This assignment allowed us to explore the significance of data in the e-commerce industry.

Second Assignment

The second assignment was a more challenging as we delved into the world of databases. Our task was to create a comprehensive database for a restaurant, encompassing customer information, bookings, and orders. To achieve this, we utilized Laragon and MySQL. One of the most demanding aspects of the assignment was designing the database structure. We had to create UML diagrams, conceptualize and implement logical models, and ultimately translate them into the physical data model. This assignment was a great opportunity to put our database design skills to the test.
